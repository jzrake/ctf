Mara magnetized, relativistic astrophysics

Overview

Mara is a tubulence code for astrophysical hydrodynamics and relativistic MHD.

It is the result of its author's graduate study at New York Univerity between Summer 2008 and Spring 2013, under Andrew MacFadyen.
